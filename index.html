1. STARSEED'Z

STARSEED’Z (also known as SSZ) is a 4 member international K-pop girl group under the French entertainment company, Seedbox Entertainment. We work with many producers, realisators, songwritters, choreographers,... around the world (French, American, Thaï, Korean, Italian,...). We participated at various event around France like Paris Manga, Japan Expo Marseille, Vannes Sekaï Festival,... We aim to make a mark on the international scene by conveying our unique identity and musical vision enriched by our cultural diversity.
